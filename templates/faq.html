Frequently Asked Questions

FestiViews is dedicated to providing a range of important information and reviews for a host of music festivals. Users can search for festivals to find all important lineup information, links to tickets, or even just to find out if the festival is going ahead this year.

Each festival on the site has its own 'hub', where all the most important information can be found, as well as user submitted reviews which can help others decide whether or not to attend a future iteration of a festival or not.

To add a review to any of the festival hubs, you'll need to first create an account and ensure you are signed in. You can do so here

As long as you are signed into the same account as when you posted the review, you can edit or even delete your review if you want to.

While we are doing our best to ensure that as many music festivals are on the site as possible, we won't necessarily have uploaded every single village fair or farm festival in the UK.

What you can do is contact us to request a new festival, and we'll aim to have it uploaded as soon as possible.
